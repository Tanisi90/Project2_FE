Welcome to our Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition Character Creator! Here, you can create a characters, store them in our database, peruse helpful information, all in the convenience of a single page application!

This application was developed by Brittany, Claudia, and Alex as a part of their training as Full-stack Software Engineers with Revature. It uses information from the Wizards of the Coast's System Reference Document, which is made available by the D&D 5th Edition API.
